What I’ve been building

Over the last few years my work has clustered around three themes: making it safe and easy for engineers to ship database changes, lowering the cost of running large fleets on AWS, and giving teams visibility into what their systems are actually doing.

At Traveloka I architected a Database CI/CD platform that automates SQL execution across 250+ production databases, layered with SQL linting and automated unit testing to catch deployment failures before they happen. I also led the organization-wide rollout of AWS ECS Fargate Spot across 800+ services, with a recovery system to absorb spot interruptions — together driving roughly $1.4M of annualized cost avoidance.
